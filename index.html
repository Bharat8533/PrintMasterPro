How to Proceed with Printer Setup

1

Remove all protective tapes and packaging, then connect to power.

2

Install the ink/toner cartridges carefully into their designated slots.

3

Load plain white paper into the input tray according to your model's capacity.

4

Set your preferences (Language, Date, Region) on the control panel.

Wireless Connectivity Guide

  • Enable Wi-Fi from the Wireless Settings menu.
  • Select your SSID (Network Name) from the discovered list.
  • Enter your security key/password accurately.
  • Download the mobile app to print directly from your smartphone.

Fix "Printer Offline" Errors

Is your printer refusing to respond? Try these quick-fix steps before calling for service.

1. Check Connections

Ensure the USB cable is tight or the Wi-Fi signal is strong at the printer's location.

2. Toggle Online Mode

Go to 'Devices and Printers', right-click your printer, and uncheck 'Use Printer Offline'.

3. Clear Print Queue

Cancel all pending documents that might be blocking the print spooler service.

4. Update Drivers

Outdated software is the #1 cause of offline errors. Always use official manufacturer drivers.

5. Paper Jam Check

Inspect the internal rollers for tiny scraps of paper that might trigger a sensor error.
